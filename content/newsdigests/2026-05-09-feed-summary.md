## Key Highlights
- Two pointed essays from the HN front page argue that the *AI chatbot* has replaced the carousel as the trendy-but-useless website fixture clients demand, and that AI-generated key art now signals low social literacy more than effort saved.
- Two notable open-source repos hit GitHub Trending: Anthropic's `financial-services` reference agents/skills bundle, and Addy Osmani's `agent-skills` — a 21-skill workflow library encoding senior-engineer practices into AI coding agents.

## Analysis & Opinion
### [All My Clients Wanted a Carousel, Now It's an AI Chatbot](https://adele.pages.casa/md/blog/all-my-clients-wanted-a-carousel-now-it-s-an-ai-chatbot.md) — Hacker News
A web designer's field note on client psychology: the same clients who admit chatbots annoy them and that they close them instantly still demand one on their own site. Like the carousel before it, the AI chatbot is a perception artifact — sites without one feel "unfinished" — and real visitors will scroll past it in half a second looking for a phone number.

### [AI Art and Social Perception](https://mccue.dev/pages/5-8-26-ai-art) — Hacker News
Ethan McCue argues using AI-generated key art is a negative-EV move: best case the audience doesn't notice, worst case they read you as socially illiterate, and almost no one assumes you spent meaningful effort on prompting. He recommends crude photo edits, hand-drawn doodles, or commissioning a real artist as strictly better signals.

## New Products & Tools
### [Anthropic financial-services repository](https://github.com/anthropics/financial-services) — GitHub Trending
A reference repo of agents, skills, and data connectors targeting financial-services workflows, shipped in two modes: Claude Cowork plugins for in-product use, and Claude Managed Agents API for headless deployment behind a workflow engine. Includes named agents for coverage & advisory (Pitch, Meeting Prep), research & modeling (Market Researcher, Earnings Reviewer, Model Builder), and fund-admin/finance ops (Valuation Reviewer, GL Reconciler).

### [Agent Skills](https://github.com/addyosmani/agent-skills) — GitHub Trending
Addy Osmani's collection of 21 structured engineering skills for AI coding agents, organized across six phases: Define, Plan, Build, Verify, Review, and Ship. The pitch is that skills encode the workflows and quality gates senior engineers actually use — spec writing, testing, security review, deployment — so agents follow disciplined practice instead of taking shortcuts.
