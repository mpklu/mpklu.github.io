## New Products & Tools

### [MiniMax M2.7 Advances Scalable Agentic Workflows on NVIDIA Platforms](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/minimax-m2-7-advances-scalable-agentic-workflows-on-nvidia-platforms-for-complex-ai-applications/) — NVIDIA
MiniMax releases M2.7 with open weights, building on the M2 family's sparse mixture-of-experts architecture — 256 local experts, 8 active per token, 200K context length, all at a 4.3% activation rate to keep inference costs low. NVIDIA and open-source communities integrated optimized kernels into vLLM and SGLang, achieving up to 2.7x throughput improvements on Blackwell GPUs within a month.
