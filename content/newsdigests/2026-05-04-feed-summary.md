## Research

### [AI shows its skills in the emergency room](https://www.therundown.ai/p/ai-shows-its-skills-in-the-emergency-room) — The Rundown
Harvard researchers ran OpenAI's o1-preview against two attending physicians on 76 emergency-department cases, scoring at the triage stage where information is sparsest. The model came in at **67.1% diagnostic accuracy versus 55.3% and 50.0%** for the human doctors, and independent reviewers couldn't tell AI-generated diagnoses from human ones. The standout case: o1-preview surfaced a rare flesh-eating infection 12–24 hours before the attending caught it.
