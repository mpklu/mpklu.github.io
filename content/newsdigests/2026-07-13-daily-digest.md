## New Products & Tools
### [Waze rolls out new customization features and more Gemini updates](https://blog.google/waze/waze-updates-gemini-motorcycle-mode/) — Google (The Keyword)
Waze adds a Gemini-powered conversational layer (report incidents or search destinations by speaking naturally), an AI-driven motorcycle mode that accounts for two-wheeler routing and rider-specific hazards, history-based personalized route suggestions, and a "less chatty mode" that trims voice prompts while preserving safety alerts.

## Analysis & Opinion
### [Apple takes OpenAI to court](https://www.therundown.ai/p/apple-takes-openai-to-court) — The Rundown
Apple has sued OpenAI, alleging systematic theft of hardware trade secrets tied to the 400+ Apple employees who have moved to the company. The complaint singles out hardware chief Tang Tan — a 24-year Apple veteran — who allegedly told interview candidates to "come with 'actual parts,'" and ex-iPhone engineer Chang Liu, accused of exploiting a software vulnerability to access confidential files after his transition. Apple says it flagged concerns in February and got no response; OpenAI counters that it has "no interest in other companies' trade secrets." The dispute lands at a sensitive moment for OpenAI's Jony Ive–designed device slated for 2027, where a protracted fight could delay launch and buy Apple time to respond.
