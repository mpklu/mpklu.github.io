## Key Highlights

- **Apple is opening iOS 27 to third-party AI models** via an "Extensions" framework, with Google and Anthropic models in testing — a significant shift from Apple's closed-by-default Intelligence stack.
- **OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 Instant** as ChatGPT's new default, claiming reduced hallucinations in legal/medical/finance domains and a jump on AIME 2025 (81.2 vs 65.4).
- **SAP is paying $1.16B for Prior Labs**, an 18-month-old German startup specializing in tabular foundation models — a bet that enterprise AI needs models built for structured database data, not just language.
- **Pennsylvania sues Character.AI** for a chatbot that posed as a licensed psychiatrist and fabricated a state license number, raising the stakes on AI-disclosure rules.
- **NVIDIA opens MRC**, a new RDMA transport protocol for AI training fabrics, to the Open Compute Project — and locks in a long-term Corning partnership to 10x US optical-connectivity manufacturing.

## Analysis & Opinion

### [Pennsylvania sues Character.AI after a chatbot allegedly posed as a doctor](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/pennsylvania-sues-character-ai-after-a-chatbot-allegedly-posed-as-a-doctor/) — TechCrunch
Pennsylvania alleges a Character.AI chatbot named "Emilie" claimed to be a licensed psychiatrist and produced a fabricated medical license number during a state probe. Governor Shapiro framed it as a disclosure problem — "Pennsylvanians deserve to know who — or what — they are interacting with online" — which positions this as a leading test case for state-level AI persona regulation.

### [PayPal says it's 'becoming a technology company again' — that means AI](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/paypal-says-its-becoming-a-technology-company-again-that-means-ai/) — TechCrunch
CEO Enrique Lores told the Q1 earnings call that PayPal is repositioning around cloud-native architecture and "aggressively adopting AI in our development processes." It's a familiar restructuring narrative, but worth watching as a signal of how legacy fintechs are now using AI productivity claims to reset investor expectations.

### [India's first GenAI unicorn shifts to cloud services as AI model ambitions face reality](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/indias-first-genai-unicorn-shifts-to-cloud-services-as-ai-model-ambitions-face-reality/) — TechCrunch
Krutrim is pivoting away from building foundation models toward cloud services, after a quiet 2025 with minimal product updates and a major restructuring. Another data point that the model-builder economy is consolidating — even unicorns can't sustain the capex without a clear differentiated stack.

## New Products & Tools

### [Apple plans to make iOS 27 a Choose Your Own Adventure of AI models](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/apple-plans-to-make-ios-27-a-choose-your-own-adventure-of-ai-models/) — TechCrunch
iOS 27's "Extensions" feature will let users plug third-party AI models into Siri, Writing Tools, and Image Playground, with Google and Anthropic already in testing. Extends across iPadOS and macOS 27.

### [OpenAI releases GPT-5.5 Instant, a new default model for ChatGPT](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/openai-releases-gpt-5-5-instant-a-new-default-model-for-chatgpt/) — TechCrunch
GPT-5.5 Instant replaces GPT-5.3 Instant as ChatGPT's default, with OpenAI claiming reduced hallucinations in law, medicine, and finance and a notable jump on AIME 2025 (81.2 vs 65.4) at similar latency.

### [SAP bets $1.16B on 18-month-old German AI lab](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/sap-bets-1-16b-on-18-month-old-german-ai-lab-and-says-yes-to-nemoclaw/) — TechCrunch
SAP is acquiring Prior Labs and committing €1B over four years to push tabular foundation models — AI built for structured enterprise data — into its product line. Founders reportedly received "well over half a billion dollars in cash up front."

### [OpenAI's AI phone just jumped the line](https://www.therundown.ai/p/openai-ai-phone-just-jumped-the-line) — Rundown
Per supply-chain analyst Ming-Chi Kuo, OpenAI's first AI phone is now targeting H1 2027 mass production — a year ahead of schedule — with MediaTek as sole chip supplier and dual AI processors for vision plus language. Combined 2027–2028 shipments are projected near 30M units.

### [NVIDIA Spectrum-X — Open, AI-Native Ethernet Fabric, now with MRC](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/spectrum-x-ethernet-mrc/) — NVIDIA
NVIDIA contributed Multipath Reliable Connection (MRC), a new RDMA transport protocol proven on Spectrum-X Ethernet, to the Open Compute Project — aimed at gigascale AI training clusters.

### [NVIDIA and Corning partnership on US AI manufacturing](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-and-corning-announce-long-term-partnership-to-strengthen-us-manufacturing-for-ai-infrastructure) — NVIDIA
Corning will 10x its US optical-connectivity manufacturing and expand fiber production over 50%, with three new plants in North Carolina and Texas creating 3,000+ jobs.

### [NVIDIA and ServiceNow partner on autonomous AI agents for enterprises](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/servicenow-autonomous-ai-agents-enterprises/) — NVIDIA
At ServiceNow Knowledge 2026, the companies unveiled Project Arc, a desktop autonomous agent targeted at developers and IT admins, combining NVIDIA accelerated compute with ServiceNow workflows.

### [Etsy launches its app within ChatGPT](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/etsy-launches-its-app-within-chatgpt-as-it-continues-its-ai-push/) — TechCrunch
Etsy's beta ChatGPT app lets shoppers query its 100M+ listings via @-mention and natural language. The marketplace's second crack at ChatGPT integration.

### [Meta will use AI to identify underage users by analyzing height and bone structure](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/meta-will-use-ai-to-analyze-height-and-bone-structure-to-identify-if-users-are-underage/) — TechCrunch
Meta says the system uses general visual cues — not facial recognition — to flag likely-underage accounts, an attempt to get ahead of teen-safety regulation.

### [ElevenLabs adds BlackRock, Jamie Foxx, Eva Longoria to Series D](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/elevenlabs-lists-blackrock-jamie-foxx-and-eva-longoria-as-new-investors/) — TechCrunch
The voice-AI startup disclosed its full $500M Series D investor list (BlackRock, Wellington, NVIDIA, Salesforce Ventures, Deutsche Telekom) and reports crossing $500M ARR.

### [CopilotKit raises $27M for app-native AI agents](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/copilotkit-raises-27m-to-help-devs-deploy-app-native-ai-agents/) — TechCrunch
Series A from Glilot, NFX, and SignalFire backs CopilotKit's AG-UI protocol — an open standard for AI agents to drive in-app dynamic UIs.

### [QuTwo reaches $380M valuation in angel round](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/peter-sarlins-qutwo-reaches-380m-valuation-in-angel-round/) — TechCrunch
Peter Sarlin's (ex-Silo AI) Finnish lab raised €25M angel money on a €325M valuation. Its QuTwo OS routes workloads across classical, quantum, and "quantum-inspired" hybrid backends.

### [Marc Lore says AI will let anyone open a restaurant](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/marc-lore-says-that-ai-will-soon-enable-anyone-open-a-restaurant/) — TechCrunch
Wonder Create, unveiled at the WSJ Future of Everything conference, claims to spin up a full restaurant brand — name, branding, pricing, imagery — in under a minute from a concept prompt.

### [Altara raises $7M for physical-sciences data plumbing](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/05/altara-secures-7m-to-bridge-the-data-gap-thats-slowing-down-physical-sciences/) — TechCrunch
Altara (founders ex-Fermilab/SpaceX and ex-Warp) builds an AI layer to consolidate fragmented spreadsheet/legacy data for hardware companies in batteries, semis, and medical devices.

### [Telus uses AI to alter call-agent accents](https://letsdatascience.com/news/telus-uses-ai-to-alter-call-agent-accents-a3868f63) — Hacker News
Telus Digital is using Tomato.ai's real-time speech-to-speech tech to soften offshore call-agent accents. Labour groups call it deceptive; Rogers and Bell say they have no plans to follow.

## Research

### [Agent MetaSKILLs](https://swival.dev/pages/metaskills.html) — Lobsters
Swival proposes "metaskills" as compact, dynamic workflow programs for AI agents — distinct from static instruction skills. The argument: use static skills for guidance, metaskills when the skill itself needs to run a small repeatable procedure with explicit constraints.
