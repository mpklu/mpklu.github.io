## Market Outlook

Markets closed essentially flat despite an intensely volatile session dominated by Trump's Iran ultimatum and sticky inflation data pushing rate cut expectations into late 2027 — the VIX jump to 25.78 tells the real story beneath the calm surface. The divergence between safe-haven flows (gold at $4,734, DXY sliding to 99.61) and resilient equity indices suggests investors are hedging rather than capitulating, with the $373B Buffett cash hoard and Druckenmiller's aggressive buying signaling smart money sees opportunity amid the chaos. All eyes turn to Thursday's CPI print and TSMC earnings on April 16, which together will test whether the inflation-geopolitics double bind loosens or tightens further heading into mega-cap earnings season.
