## Market Outlook

Markets drifted lower Tuesday in a classic pre-catalyst de-risking session — the S&P 500 eased 0.14% while speculative corners took real damage (Circle -6.7%, Rocket Lab -5.7%, Oklo -5.4%), even as NVIDIA's +2.8% gain showed the AI-leadership trade intact. The setup into next week is unusually dense: Alphabet reports tonight, Intel tomorrow, and Meta, SoFi, Apple, and Amazon all land before the July 28–29 FOMC meeting, where futures now price a meaningful ~46% chance of a hike. Newly announced tariffs on 60 countries and oil pushing $86 on Middle East tensions add inflation pressure that complicates the Fed's calculus and warrants attention on rate-sensitive small caps, which lagged again today. With six watchlist names reporting within eight days and a live Fed decision behind them, Alphabet's cloud growth number tonight is the first real test of whether Q2 big-tech earnings can carry the market through a hawkish-leaning week.
