## Overnight Developments

### [Global Semiconductor & AI Valuation Rout Slams Asia](https://asia.nikkei.com/business/markets/japanese-stocks-plunge-as-ai-deleverage-sends-kioxia-down-16) — Nikkei Asia / CNBC
The Philadelphia Semiconductor index fell **4.3%** overnight as investors aggressively unwound leverage from AI and tech trades on concerns that valuations can't be justified by earnings growth. In Japan, Kioxia crashed **16%**, Tokyo Electron **-8.1%**, and SoftBank **-9%**; SK Hynix's US-listed shares plunged **13%**. The deleveraging cascaded across the region — Nikkei -4.14%, Shanghai -3.02%, Hang Seng -1.78%.
Affected sectors: Technology, Semiconductors, AI

### [US Completes Sixth Night of Strikes on Iran; Retaliation Widens](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/17/world/live-news/iran-war-trump) — CNN
The US military completed a sixth consecutive night of airstrikes on Iranian targets overnight. Iran retaliated with strikes on Qatar, Kuwait, Jordan, Bahrain, and Syria, with 7–9 casualties reported. No de-escalation signals from either side and the ceasefire has broken down — a setup for higher oil prices and a risk-off tone.
Affected sectors: Energy, Defense, Transportation

### [White House Announces 25% Brazil Tariffs, Forced-Labor Tariffs on 60 Countries](https://www.marketplace.org/story/2026/07/16/as-trump-announces-new-tariffs-retailers-frontload-imports) — Marketplace / USTR

Affected sectors: Retail, Manufacturing, Agriculture
