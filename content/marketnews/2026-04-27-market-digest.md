## Market Outlook

The tape went sideways into the most consequential earnings week of the year — META, GOOG, AMZN report Wednesday, AAPL Thursday — and the calmness is itself information: VIX dropped 3.6% to 18 with futures pricing a 100% Fed hold tomorrow. The day's sharpest signal was inside AI infrastructure, where the Microsoft–OpenAI exclusivity unwinding, Qualcomm's chip deal, and parallel +4% to +6.5% pops in NVDA, OKLO, NNE, and SMR show capital rotating from "who builds the model" to "who powers and houses it" — a shift Druckenmiller and Chamath are both betting on with Bloom Energy. With Buffett's indicator at 227% and Marks warning on private credit, the bull case rests almost entirely on Mag 7 print quality. **What to watch:** Wednesday after the close — META, GOOG, and AMZN guidance on AI capex will set the tone for the rest of Q2.
