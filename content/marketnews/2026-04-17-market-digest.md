## Market Outlook

Today was a textbook geopolitical relief rally: oil crashed 7%, every index rallied, small caps led, VIX melted, and the most speculative names (MSTR +12%, SMR +10%) did what speculative names do when risk appetite returns. The more interesting signal is under the surface — **Buffett at a record cash pile, Wood sitting out the rally, Marks warning on private credit, and Dalio flagging capital outflows** — a growing chorus of the cautious on the same week Tom Lee calls an all-clear. Next week is the real test: UNH (Tuesday), INTC (Wednesday), and the Mag 7 cluster (GOOG/META/AMZN next Wednesday) land ahead of the April 28-29 FOMC decision, all while INTC sits at its 52-week high and Mag 7 crowds the top decile of their ranges. The setup rewards resilient guidance and punishes even mild disappointment, so earnings breadth — not just index level — is what warrants attention into month-end.
