### [TSMC Q1 profit surges 58% to record on AI chip demand](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/tsmc-q1-profit-58-percent-ai-chip-demand-record.html) — CNBC

## Market Outlook

Markets inched higher across the board as TSMC's 58% Q1 profit surge and receding Iran tensions more than offset tariff saber-rattling with Beijing, pushing S&P and NASDAQ to fresh intraday records. The next two weeks carry outsized weight — FOMC decision April 28, UNH (Apr 21) and INTC (Apr 23) kicking off earnings, followed by the mega-cap cluster of META/GOOG/AMZN/SOFI on April 29 and AAPL on April 30. Under the surface, the rotation Cramer flagged is real: RKLB +12% on a $190M hypersonic contract while SMR dropped 7% on lack of data-center customer wins, and Dimon's warning on private-credit vulnerabilities warrants attention even as equity indices make highs. INTC's push above its 52-week high ahead of earnings and Intel's 240% move off April 2025 lows suggests the semiconductor turnaround trade is now fully priced — leaving little margin for disappointment. The path of least resistance favors equities into month-end, but with the FOMC, 60% of mega-cap earnings, and the May 14 Xi-Trump summit compressed into a ten-day window, positioning discipline matters more than directional conviction.
