## Overnight Developments

No single black-swan catalyst overnight, but a confluence of macro pressure is weighing on global risk assets. The selloff appears driven by:

- **Sticky inflation backdrop** — [April CPI came in at 3.8%](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/12/cpi-inflation-april-2026--.html), keeping the "higher for longer" rate narrative alive and pressuring rate-sensitive growth names.

- **[Middle East tensions](https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/13/world/live-news/trump-iran-war-news)** remain elevated with the US blockade of Iranian ports and a fragile Israel-Hezbollah ceasefire.
- **Consolidation pressure** after the prior session closed at record highs — profit-taking compounded the overnight risk-off tone.

Affected sectors: broad-based weakness, with tech (NASDAQ -0.76%) leading the downside.
