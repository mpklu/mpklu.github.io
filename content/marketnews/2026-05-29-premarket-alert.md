## Overnight Developments

### [Tentative U.S.–Iran Deal on Strait of Hormuz and Nuclear Talks](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-may-29-2026) — TheStreet / Bloomberg

The U.S. and Iran have reportedly reached a tentative agreement to reopen the Strait of Hormuz and launch a 60-day window of nuclear negotiations, pending final approval from the Trump administration. The deal would meaningfully ease Middle East tensions and strip geopolitical premium out of oil markets, a tailwind for energy-sensitive sectors that import fuel and a headwind for crude producers.

Affected sectors: Energy, Utilities, Transportation
