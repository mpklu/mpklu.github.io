## Market Outlook

Equities rallied on geopolitical relief — Iran's halt to strikes on Israel sent the VIX down more than 12% and reignited the chip trade, with Intel exploding 13.8% on Google TPU and Foxconn deals while TSMC, SMCI and Super Micro all jumped. The move underscores how tightly sentiment is still tethered to headline risk, and beneath the enthusiasm a hotter-than-expected May jobs report has quietly flipped the rates narrative: swaps now lean toward a Fed hike by year-end, and the 10-year backed up to 4.55%. That tension — AI-driven risk appetite versus a market suddenly fearing tighter policy — warrants attention. The decisive catalyst is Wednesday's May CPI, landing just ahead of the June 16–17 FOMC; an upside surprise would harden hike fears and test today's optimism.
