## Overnight Developments

### [U.S. Launches Strikes on Iran After Apache Downed in Strait of Hormuz](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/09/trump-iran-helicopter-hormuz-strait.html) — CNBC / Bloomberg
CENTCOM began "self-defense strikes" against Iranian air defense, ground control, and radar sites near the Strait of Hormuz at 5 p.m. ET Tuesday, in response to the downing of a U.S. Army Apache helicopter. Iran retaliated overnight against U.S. bases — Jordan said it intercepted five Iranian missiles, while Bahrain and Kuwait activated air defenses against "hostile aerial targets." With Iran restricting tanker traffic through Hormuz — a chokepoint for ~25% of seaborne oil — WTI crude pushed toward **$89/bbl** and global equities sold off.
Affected sectors: Energy, Defense, Transportation/Logistics, Financials
