## Overnight Developments

### [US Blockades Iran, Escalates Strait of Hormuz Conflict](https://www.cnn.com/2026/07/14/world/live-news/iran-war-trump) — CNN, NPR
The US military began a naval blockade of Iranian ports on July 14 and struck military targets across Iran (Bushehr, Chah Bahar, Jask, Konarak, Abu Musa, Bandar Abbas). Iran has vowed to assert control over the Strait of Hormuz, and European airlines have been directed to avoid Gulf airspace. Crude oil jumped ~3.6% to roughly $74/barrel, and global government bond yields rose 4–5 basis points on escalation fears.
Affected sectors: Energy, Shipping/Transportation, Defense.

### Semiconductor Selloff on SK Hynix Miss
Separately, memory-chip weakness is pressuring the semiconductor complex. SK Hynix guided Q2 profit ~8% below consensus on slow HBM4 shipments and fell ~15% in Korea, dragging equipment and memory names (LRCX, AMAT, WDC, ON all down 4–6% pre-market). ASML bucked the trend, raising full-year guidance on AI chip demand. Watch chip-exposed names on the watchlist (NVDA, TSM, INTC, SMCI).
