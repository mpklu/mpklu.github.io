## Overnight Developments

### [Trump Pauses "Project Freedom" as US–Iran Near Framework Peace Deal](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/06/european-stocks-hormuz-de-escalation-markets-trump.html) — CNBC / Bloomberg / Axios
President Trump announced he is pausing "Project Freedom" — the US plan to escort shipping through the Strait of Hormuz — citing "Great Progress" toward a Complete and Final Agreement with Iran. Axios reports Washington and Tehran are edging toward a one-page framework to end hostilities and reopen Iranian ports, effectively lifting the US blockade after a roughly 10-week conflict. Risk-on response is broad: European indices surged (DAX +2.80%, FTSE +2.32%, CAC ~+3%), US futures jumped, and crude oil sold off sharply on easing supply-disruption fears.
Affected sectors: Energy (down on lower oil), Defense (down), Airlines, Shipping, Tech/Semis, Financials, Consumer Discretionary (up on risk-on).
