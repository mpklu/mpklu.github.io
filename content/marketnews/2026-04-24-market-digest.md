## Market Outlook

Friday's tape told a narrow story: a semiconductor-and-AI bid pulled the **NASDAQ up 1.63% to a record** while the Dow's fractional red hid a sharp rotation out of speculative nuclear/space names (**OKLO -9.6%, SMR -8.6%, RKLB -7.2%, NNE -6.1%**) and into AI infrastructure (**NVDA +4.4%, TSM +4.9%, SMCI +6.0%**). Intel's post-earnings extension, the SOX's 18-day win streak, and Alphabet's $40B Anthropic commitment all reinforce that AI capex is widening rather than peaking — even as Howard Marks and Ray Dalio warn that the current tape is anything but "on sale." With the **VIX at 18.7** and the S&P at record highs, the week ahead is the single most consequential of the quarter: Tuesday's FOMC (hold expected), then **AMZN, GOOG, META on April 29 and AAPL on April 30** — any one miss warrants attention, and a synchronized beat would likely mark the next breakout leg. The standout catalyst to watch is whether the mega-cap earnings cohort can validate the **$135B+ META and $40B GOOG** AI-capex commitments with commensurate cloud and ads acceleration.
