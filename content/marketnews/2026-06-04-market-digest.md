### [Broadcom miss triggers semiconductor rotation](https://247wallst.com/investing/2026/06/04/stock-market-live-june-4-2026-sp-500-spy-fall-as-broadcom-sinks/) — 24/7 Wall St

## Market Outlook

The session was defined by rotation, not direction: the Dow tagged a record (+1.73%) and the Russell 2000 rallied (+1.45%) while the Nasdaq slipped fractionally, as a 14% Broadcom drop and weak CrowdStrike guidance knocked the AI/semis complex and money flowed into healthcare, cyclicals, and small caps. That breadth shift — UNH +5.2% on a BofA upgrade, TSM and Tempus AI defying the chip selloff — suggests the market is testing whether leadership can broaden beyond a handful of mega-cap names, a healthy development if it sticks. With the VIX back to 15.4 and an Israel–Lebanon ceasefire pulling premium out of oil and yields, sentiment warrants cautious optimism rather than alarm. The dominant catalyst now sits squarely on Friday: May nonfarm payrolls (forecast 102K vs prior 85K, unemployment seen ticking to 4.4%) land just 12 days before the June 16–17 FOMC, and a hot print would reinforce a hold. Watch whether today's rotation has legs into the jobs number — that, more than any single stock, will set the tone for next week.
