## Breaking News & Macro Events

### [Oil Surges on Middle East Escalation; Houthi Attacks on Saudi Tankers](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/23/fed-interest-rate-odds-oil-jobless-claims.html) — CNBC

### [Tech Earnings Disappoint; Market Sells Off on AI Capex Concerns](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/23/fed-interest-rate-odds-oil-jobless-claims.html) — CNBC

## Market Outlook

Equities took a broad hit today, with the Nasdaq off 2.15% and the VIX up nearly 12% as a rare combination of catalysts hit at once: Alphabet's capex-driven earnings reaction reignited doubts about AI payback, while a Red Sea tanker attack sent Brent above $100 and drove the 10-year yield to a fresh 2026 high near 4.70%. The mix matters — higher oil and higher yields pressure the very valuations that have carried the megacap-tech rally, and a fresh 50% tariff salvo on Canadian goods adds policy uncertainty on top. With a dense earnings calendar (UPS, SoFi and Meta next week; Apple, Amazon, MSTR and Tempus the week after) and an FOMC decision landing July 28-29, the setup warrants caution rather than dip-buying reflexes. Watch whether energy's spike proves transitory or sticky — that single variable will shape both the inflation narrative and the Fed's tone into next week.
