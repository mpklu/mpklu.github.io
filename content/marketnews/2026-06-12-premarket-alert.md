## Overnight Developments

### [U.S.-Iran Peace Deal Breakthrough](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-12-2026) — TheStreet
President Trump canceled planned strikes on Iran and announced a peace deal — expected to be finalized as soon as Sunday — that would end the conflict and reopen the Strait of Hormuz. The framework reportedly pairs a U.S. commitment to lift oil sanctions with Iran's pledge to reopen the trade route within 30 days. The de-escalation triggered a global risk-on rally across Asia and Europe overnight.
Affected sectors: Energy, Transportation, Aerospace, Global Trade

### [Oil Prices Collapse on De-escalation](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-12-2026) — TheStreet
WTI crude fell ~4% to roughly $84/barrel and Brent dropped a similar amount to ~$87 on signs of an imminent peace deal. Lower energy prices ease inflation fears and support consumer and corporate margins — a tailwind for cyclicals, airlines, and industrials.
Affected sectors: Energy, Airlines, Consumer, Industrials

### [SpaceX Debuts in Record IPO](https://www.thestreet.com/stock-market-today/stock-market-today-dow-jones-sp-500-nasdaq-updates-june-12-2026) — TheStreet
SpaceX went public at $135/share (~$1.77 trillion valuation), raising ~$75 billion in what is being called the largest IPO in history. The debut underscored strong investor appetite for growth assets amid the improving geopolitical backdrop.
Affected sectors: Aerospace, Technology, Capital Markets
