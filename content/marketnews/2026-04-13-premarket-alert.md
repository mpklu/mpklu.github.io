### [Trump Announces Strait of Hormuz Blockade as Iran Talks Collapse](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/12/stock-market-today-live-updates.html) — CNBC

The move is the latest escalation in the 45-day US-Iran conflict, with Iran's Revolutionary Guards warning that military vessels approaching the strait would be considered a ceasefire breach. The IEA has described this as the most significant oil supply shock in market history.
