## Overnight Developments

### [Iran Rejects Trump's "Project Freedom" Hormuz Plan](https://www.euronews.com/business/2026/05/04/oil-markets-lower-as-trump-vows-to-help-ships-leave-strait-of-hormuz) — Euronews / CNBC
Iran has rejected the Trump administration's "Project Freedom" plan, which proposed US naval escort to help trapped tankers exit the Strait of Hormuz beginning Monday. The standoff keeps roughly 20% of global oil trade impaired and signals the disruption — already the largest oil supply shock in recorded history per industry estimates — will drag further into Q2.
Affected sectors: Energy, Transportation, Shipping, Defense, Industrials.

### [Trump Strengthens Section 232 Tariffs on Steel, Aluminum, Copper](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2026/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-strengthens-tariffs-on-steel-aluminum-and-copper-imports/) — White House
A weekend proclamation expands Section 232 tariffs to cover the full declared value of imported metals products under revised calculation rules, citing national security. Domestic mills benefit; downstream manufacturers (autos, machinery, construction) face higher input costs.
Affected sectors: Industrials, Manufacturing, Autos, Steel, Aluminum.
